Selection Progress

As a moderator, you play an important role in the selection process of our students. Here are the steps you should follow:

  1. Review the applications of each student carefully.
  2. Shortlist the candidates who meet the eligibility criteria.
  3. Conduct interviews with the shortlisted candidates to assess their knowledge and skills.
  4. Submit your recommendations for the final selection of candidates.

If you have any questions or concerns about the selection process, please feel free to contact us.

Selection Progress

As a teacher, you have the opportunity to contribute to the selection process of our students. Here are the steps you should follow:

  1. Review the applications of each student carefully.
  2. Provide feedback on the academic performance and potential of each candidate.
  3. Conduct assessments and tests to evaluate the knowledge and skills of the candidates.
  4. Collaborate with the moderator to finalize the selection of candidates.

If you have any questions or concerns about the selection process, please feel free to contact us.
